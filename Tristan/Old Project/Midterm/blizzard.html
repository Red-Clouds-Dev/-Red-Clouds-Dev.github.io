What is Blizzard? Blizzard Entertainment, is an American video game developer and publisher based in Irvine, California, and a subsidiary of the American company Activision Blizzard.

Blizzard Entertainment has one of the biggest multiplayer online role-playing game in the world. World of Warcraft as well as Diablo, StarCraft, and Overwatch. The games are run through the online game service Battle.net.
