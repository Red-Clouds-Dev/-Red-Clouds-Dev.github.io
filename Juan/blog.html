Motivation and The Journey of Learning Japanese

Posted on 11/27/2023

Introduction:

I've noticed a big shift in my motivation over the last few weeks. My initial motivation came from the thrill of taking on new challenges and the satisfaction of learning new abilities...

But I realized that I've been too preoccupied with getting things done quickly, like learning how to write simple sentences in Japanese or grasp basic phrases I now intend to study Japanese more thoroughly, not just as a language but also as a means of gaining access to a vibrant and rich culture.

The Evolution of Motivation:

My motivation for learning Japanese was the excitement of delving into a foreign language and culture. My strategy started to focus more on measurable outcomes over time. I now realize that, although these accomplishments are significant, they shouldn't take precedence over the more profound and satisfying aspects of genuinely grasping and enjoying a foreign language.

Changes in the Learning Process:

As I think back on my teaching strategies, I've noticed a shift. Things that used to require hours of focused study now appear to take less time. This got me thinking: have I learned more effectively, or have I lost the depth and interest that drove my previous language studies?

Embracing Japanese Language Learning:

This is where I want to apply my recently found commitment to learning Japanese. Like my initial learning days, learning more Japanese is going to be a journey full of obstacles and learning experiences. Through this deeper engagement with Japanese, I hope to rediscover my passion for language learning, which excites me.

Infusing New Themes: Japanese Language and Culture:

I've made the decision to immerse myself more in Japanese language and culture in order to reignite my passion, and I'll be blogging about my experiences. Every new term and cultural understanding will present a chance for investigation and exchange. This method will not only give my routine a new perspective, but it will also spark my interest in learning again.

Revisiting Past Learning with a New Perspective:

My past experiences with learning Japanese have always been both enjoyable and challenging. I intend to go over these earlier lessons with this fresh perspective. How might a more thorough comprehension of the language and culture yield fresh perspectives? I am excited to investigate this intriguing possibility.

Learning Japanese as a Tool for Personal Growth:

I've always believed that picking up a new language improves my perspective and helps me comprehend various viewpoints, going beyond simple vocabulary and grammar learning. I want to use these experiences to advance both myself and my thinking as I learn more Japanese.

Conclusion:

I'm starting this adventure to regain the passion of learning as well as to advance my language abilities. With my renewed dedication to learning Japanese, I intend to improve my language skills as well as rekindle my enthusiasm for lifelong learning and cross-cultural travel.

Call to Action:

I'd like to ask everyone to travel with me. Talk about your experiences breaking through language hurdles, picking up new languages, or uncovering your true language study inclinations. Together, let's grow and inspire one another in this fascinating new era of language learning and cultural exploration.

Thank you for reading!

Reflecting and Preparing: My Year in Computer Programming and Science

Posted on 11/25/2023

As the year winds down, it feels like just yesterday that we were ringing in the New Year. Time has flown by, and it's that season again where we reflect on the past months...

This year, much like many others, I've tread carefully due to the global circumstances. It wasn't a year for large-scale tech conferences or extensive collaborative projects, but rather a period of cautious navigation and thoughtful planning.

I've felt a little limited in the field of computer science and programming. There wasn't the normal rush of hackathons, coding marathons, and group coding sessions, which made me feel like my potential wasn't reached. But rather than seeing this as a setback, I see it as inspiration for the upcoming year.

I am filled with eagerness and resolve as I look ahead. I want to fully commit to learning programming and computer science, pushing the boundaries and taking advantage of every chance that presents itself. I'm prepared to make up for this year's lack of activity, whether it is by working on creative projects, investigating cutting-edge technology, or participating in community-driven programming activities.

I can't wait to share this technical journey with you. As I prepare for an exciting year filled with coding, creativity, and education, follow along for frequent updates. I am so grateful for your involvement and support, and I can't wait to share my learnings and experiences with you.

Cheers to another year full of innovative coding, never-ending education, and deep friendships within the tech community. This year, let's make it one to remember!
